EgyptAir Flight 804 Crash

An EgyptAir plane disappeared the evening of Wednesday May 19th. The Egyptian navy found wreckage in the Mediterranean on Friday. The plane swerved quickly before it crashed into the sea. The cause is yet unknown.

Zika

The Zika virus broke out in South America in 2016 because it found a "happy situation for transmission"ref. It is only dangerous to pregnant women, as it causes birth defects like microcephaly. More than 2500 babies have been affected

Hillary Clinton's Private Email

Hillary Clinton, acting as Secretary of State, used a private email server rather than the State Department's so she didn't have to manage two email accounts. Some consider this mishandling national-security secrets. Classified information was not appropriately marked in her emails. Investigations by the intelligence community have not resulted in legal repercussions.
